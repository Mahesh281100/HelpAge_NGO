How You Can Help

Thousands of elders are without basic necessities during the harsh winters. HelpAge India’s programs helps such elders in need to get through these difficult times. Your support can provide them with blankets, medicines and shelter to survive. This New Year, while we celebrate all things new, surrounded by our loved ones…let’s not forget the old and frail, who have no one to call their own. Let’s make a resolution to spread warmth and comfort to our disadvantaged elderly. Be the hero they deserve! This New Year, let's resolve to give our elders a new lease of life. #BeTheHeroTheyDeserve!
