REC supports HelpAge to build shelter home for abandoned, destitute elders

Aug 00, 2012

Rural Electrification Corporation Limited (REC), a Govt. of India – Navratna Enterprise, has extended CSR support for Rs.3.50 crore to HelpAge India for F.Y. 2017-18, for the construction and operation of a shelter home with a wellness facility for elders at Shey-village, Leh-Ladakh, Jammu and Kashmir. The project is being undertaken at Shey village, Leh-Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir through the CSR arm of the corporation i.e. REC Foundation. With this support, HelpAge is setting up an integrated model facility for elder care.
